Welcome to MFS Exports

Based in Kannur, Kerala (India), MFS Exports is a trusted manufacturer and exporter of premium cashew kernels with over six years of industry expertise. Founded by Mr. Shereef, a dedicated entrepreneur with deep experience in the cashew and dry fruit export market across South India, our company is now expanding its footprint globally.

At MFS Exports, we are fully committed to upholding the highest standards in quality, ethics, and customer satisfaction. Our mission is to develop, produce, and deliver cashew products that exceed expectations in both taste and nutritional value.

With a robust global supply chain and long-standing relationships with trusted vendors, we are well-equipped to meet bulk demands promptly and efficiently. We currently handle and deliver up to a minimum of 500 KG of premium cashews per shipment, ensuring freshness and quality in every batch. We take pride in offering cashew kernels that are not only rich in nutrition but also handled with care throughout the supply process.
